My Home

I currently reside in Seattle, close to the University of Washington. I have lived in the city for all 24 years of my life and hope to continue doing so for some time to come.

Travel

Following 4 years of studying Biology at the University of Washington, I decided I wanted to do some travelling to both see the world and give myself some time do figure out my next career move. I went to Mexico, Belize, Guatemala, Honduras, Peru, Chile, Bolivia, Argentina, Ecuador and finally Columbia. It took a 11 months in total and was easily one of the coolest experience I've had so far in life.

A couple highlights:
  • Hiking an active volcano in the south of Chile and looking into the crater at molten lava
  • Seeing Machu Picchu twice over a 6 month time frame, one time by train and the other walking
  • Snorkled with sharks, sting rays, loggerhead turtles, eagle rays and more in the islands of Belize

Career Goals

One day I would like to be a user experience designer or a product designer/industrial designer. My hope for the future is that I can find a way to incorporate the knowledge I have from my biology degree into products usable in the medical/research fields. I think there is a lot of opportunity in that area.
